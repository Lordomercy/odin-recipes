Sam Vimes's BLT

It was about having to lift up quite a lot of crispy bacon before you found the miserable skulking vegetables. You might never notice them at all.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices wholewheat bread
  • Â½ pound of bacon
  • 4 green lettuce leaves, white parts removed
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Pepper
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Fry up the bacon over medium heat, making sure not to crowd the pan. Remove at desired crunchiness level and set aside on a plate.
  2. Fry the tomato slices in the bacon fat, around 30 seconds per side. Remove from the pan and add generous sprinkle of pepper.
  3. Slice bacon strips in half for easier assembly. If you like, add Worcestershire sauce (1-3 drops) to the bacon and mix well.
  4. Assemble the sandwich, starting with bread, lettuce, tomato, bacon, then bread again.
  5. Methodically remove the tomato and lettuce.
  6. Enjoy the BLT as nature intended.
